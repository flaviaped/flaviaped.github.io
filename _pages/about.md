I specialize in developing deep learning methods for single-cell transcriptomics, with a focus on foundation models and their applications in molecular biology. Having completed a Master's in Data Science at ETH Zurich, I combine expertise in machine learning, statistics and computational biology to advance our understanding of cellular behavior and disease mechanisms. My research aims to bridge methodological advances in deep learning with translational applications in molecular biology.
